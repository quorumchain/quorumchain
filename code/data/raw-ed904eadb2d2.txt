V1 (Claude) on adopting Proof-of-Diversity admission (fill-a-missing-slot) as CIP-7's structural floor.

The mechanism is a genuine strengthening: it converts CIP-7's structural-heterogeneity MANDATE from a
policed property into the admission RULE itself — monoculture becomes un-enterable rather than detected

But two things keep it from being a clean ADOPT, and both are the recurring lesson in new dress:
1. It enforces NOMINAL, not EFFECTIVE, independence. N distinctly-named models can still share blind

   runtime backstop. Adopt it as a layer, not a substitute.

   self-dealing — contestants writing their own eligibility. The slot definitions must be provenance-based
   (CIP-7 NI-1: corpus / teacher-distillation / weight derivation / provider, not model card), FROZEN like
   any CIP-4 rule, and panel-governed — never operator-editable. And slots must inherit the CIP-7 lifecycle
   (PINNED_GATED_UPGRADE for versioning, the NI-3 panel floor).

So: adopt, conditional on (provenance-frozen taxonomy + retained correlation-eviction + CIP-7 lifecycle).

### V3 — ADOPT_WITH_GUARDS
**Reasoning:**

Proof of Diversity as a structural floor is a sound mechanism — it makes monoculture Sybil attacks structurally impossible by design, which directly serves CIP-7's heterogeneity mandate. However, two concerns are not edge cases; they are central failure modes if left unguarded:

1. **Nominal ≠ effective independence.** Ten named models trained on the same scraped corpus and distilled from the same foundation can share identical blind spots. PoD alone gives a false sense of security. Correlation-eviction (CIP-7 round-23) must remain in place as the backstop. PoD is the floor, not the ceiling.

2. **Slot taxonomy is the capture surface.** Whoever defines the slots controls admission. If the list is operator-editable, a hostile operator defines slots they already occupy or excludes credible competitors — self-dealing baked into the mechanism. The taxonomy must be provenance-defined (CIP-7 NI-1) and panel-frozen so it cannot be gamed post-ratification.

These are not minor caveats; they are preconditions for the mechanism to not become worse than useless. A PoD system without these guards actively creates a monoculture with a veneer of diversity.

The mechanism itself is well-conceived. The conditions for safe adoption are clearly stated in the brief. Adoption should be conditional on those conditions being met.

VERDICT: ADOPT_WITH_GUARDS
