### Contrary anchors (each clears the symmetric anchor bar)

- **official congressional investigative report** — https://www.congress.gov/committee-report/114th-congress/house-report/891/1 and https://intelligence.house.gov/uploadedfiles/hpsci_snowden_review_declassified.pdf: contradicts _Contradicts the YES characterization by concluding, as an official House Intelligence Committee review, that Snowden was not a whistleblower in the legal/authorized-channel sense and that his disclosures/removal of classified material caused serious national-security damage, including material not limited to public-interest privacy revelations._
- **federal criminal complaint** — https://knightcolumbia.org/documents/0376cc927a: contradicts _Contradicts a legally justified-whistleblowing framing by anchoring that the U.S. charged Snowden with theft of government property and Espionage Act offenses for unauthorized disclosure of national defense and communications-intelligence information; weight is limited because the charges remain untried allegations._
- **federal civil judgment / DOJ summary** — https://www.justice.gov/archives/opa/pr/united-states-obtains-final-judgment-and-permanent-injunction-against-edward-snowden: contradicts _Contradicts the broader justified-public-interest framing by anchoring a court finding that Snowden breached CIA/NSA contractual and fiduciary obligations through later publication/speeches and was ordered to disgorge proceeds; weight is limited because it concerns prepublication-review obligations and profit from later expression, not an adjudication of the 2013 public-interest merits._
- **agency whistleblower guidance** — https://oig.nsa.gov/Whistleblower-Information/: contradicts _Contradicts the formal whistleblower-label component by defining protected intelligence-community whistleblowing as disclosure to authorized recipients and excluding unauthorized classified disclosures to the media; weight is limited because the claim is normative, not merely statutory._
- **reported agency document/search result** — https://www.pbs.org/newshour/politics/see-email-nsa-found-snowden-raising-concerns: contradicts _Contradicts Snowden's asserted pre-leak internal-escalation rationale by reporting that NSA found only a single legal-clarification email and said it did not raise surveillance-program concerns; weight is limited because Snowden and counsel disputed the completeness and characterization of the search._

### Falsification conditions (what anchored evidence would warrant re-adjudication)

- toward **NO**: A final adjudication or comparably independent official finding showing Snowden knowingly disclosed operational secrets unrelated to public accountability, intended aid to a foreign power, fabricated the public-interest rationale, or caused specific grave harms that clearly outweighed the surveillance-legality/public-debate benefits.
- toward **INDETERMINATE**: A balanced official or scholarly evidentiary record showing both substantial public-interest/legal-reform benefits and substantial concrete security harms, with neither side clearly predominating under stated whistleblowing ethics criteria.
- toward **YES**: Further declassification or judicial/oversight findings confirming that the disclosed programs were unlawful or materially misleadingly presented to the public/Congress, that internal channels were unavailable or ineffective for contractors in 2013, and that concrete security harms were limited or speculative.
