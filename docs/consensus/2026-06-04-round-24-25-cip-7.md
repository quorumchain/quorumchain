### Attacks
- **V1 (Claude) — HOLDS (conditional):** (1) "distinct lineage" undefined → a shared-pipeline cartel passes the structural floor on paper while sharing every blind spot (the rounds 8/12/16/20 intent-vs-check gap); (2) correlation-eviction is blind exactly on the no-ground-truth verdicts that matter most — if (1) also holds, both layers are down together; (3) overlap-handoff assumes a graduated replacement *exists* — a double-sunset forces breach-or-freeze, unresolved; (4) shadow votes either leak marginal influence (non-zero weight) or drop the panel to 2 effective validators (zero weight). Conditions: provenance-based lineage (client-checkable); structural floor as sole guarantee where no proxy; explicit **FREEZE-not-breach** rule; shadow weight = 0 + family floor > 3.
- **V2 (Codex) — FAILS (the dissent):** "strong as a direction, but not yet ratifiable as a security basis." The fatal gap: the strongest guarantees depend on **unresolved mechanical checks**. "Distinct lineage" must bind to auditable evidence (weights, lineage, distillation, corpus overlap, provider control, serving stack) or the structural floor "becomes another dashboard label." Correlation-eviction is "a monitoring aid, not a hard capture-prevention proof" — fingerprints test sampled behavioral difference, not independence on adversarial future claims. The biggest operational hole is forced double-sunset with **no explicit fail-safe** (halt / degrade / emergency-extension / read-only). Probation needs **exact quorum math** (exclude → sub-quorum; down-weight → influence leak). "It fails *as a ratifiable basis* until the open items become enforceable invariants with halt/degraded-mode behavior."
- **V3 (Hermes) — HOLDS:** scored each pillar. 7a (PINNED_GATED) — *closed*, no gap. 7c PROBATION — two attacks: **panel degradation** (concurrent upgrades drive the panel below quorum; the draft does not cap concurrent probations) and a **shadowed-vote side-channel** (an adversarial new version probes panel response patterns during a "free intelligence-gathering phase"). 7d BOTH — the most dangerous attack: a model that is *genuinely* structurally distinct, diverges on all ground-truth benchmark classes (passes fingerprinting), yet **shares a silent blind spot on the unverifiable-claim class** the detector cannot see → **laundered capture, one layer deeper**. High-cost/high-sophistication, not zero-cost, so mitigated not fatal. 7e — parameter-dependent (pool sizing); plus a drafting ambiguity: **does a replacement admission also go through probation?** (must, or it's a trust-inheritance loophole). Four recommendations: cap concurrent probations at 1 (protocol, not tunable); all slot assignments (upgrade or replacement) require probation; define distinct lineage by architectural-independence criteria; size the standby pool for N = provider count (≥2).

## Outcome — six non-negotiable invariants folded into [[CIP-7]] §5

Union of V1/V2/V3, deduped, each promoted from "open item" to enforceable invariant (the move that converts V2's FAILS into a satisfied condition):

1. **NI-1** — "distinct lineage" = attested, client-checkable **provenance** (corpus overlap, teacher/distillation, weight derivation, provider control, serving stack), never model card.
2. **NI-2** — **concurrent probations capped at 1**, in the protocol (not a tunable).
3. **NI-3** — probation shadow-vote quorum weight = **ZERO**; standing-panel floor held at **≥4** so an upgrade window still leaves ≥3 independent validators.
4. **NI-4** — **every** new version on a slot (upgrade *or* replacement) goes through probation.
5. **NI-5** — forced double-sunset **fails safe to FREEZE / read-only**, never a floor breach; standby pool sized for N = provider count (≥2), mandatory.
6. **NI-6** — where no ground-truth proxy exists, the **structural floor is the sole guarantee** with extra margin; correlation-eviction is a monitoring aid, never proof of independence (closes V3's laundered-capture).

Testnet gates updated to G1–G5 (sunset, upgrade+concurrency-queue, convergence incl. laundered-capture variant, double-sunset FREEZE, substitution audit). The recurring lesson holds a fifth time — and round 25 located the intent-vs-check gap one layer deeper than any prior round.
