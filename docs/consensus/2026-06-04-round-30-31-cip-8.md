**Convergent finding (all three):** the SRA *primitive* is sound, but the draft **overclaimed the product's security** and left load-bearing checks as open items. V2's FAILS and V3's HOLDS list nearly identical conditions; the disagreement is only whether unresolved-as-open-item is ratifiable (V1/V3: yes, as basis; V2: no, not until enforceable invariants).

### Attacks
- **V1 (Claude) — HOLDS (conditional):** (1) the notary secures the *wrong thing* — a well-formed FALSE attestation gets permanent non-repudiation; for the unverifiable class it never resolves, so the lie stands forever with a signature — risk is users reading "on chain" as "verified"; (2) the **meta-oracle is the deepest gap** — RESOLUTION's ground-truth feed is itself capturable; frozen criteria defend the judgment, not the input; (3) "resolver holds no position" is a rule with no mechanical check (off-book positions); (4) freezing **locks in ambiguity** and the "amendment before facts known" hatch just moves the fight to defining "before"; (5) evidence commitments without a disclosure rule are theater. Crucially: **KERNEL_FIRST already quarantines the deepest risk** (the notary kernel needs no oracle), so Attack 2 only bites at the gated v0.3 stage.
- **V2 (Codex) — FAILS (the dissent):** "does not yet hold as a ratification basis — the draft overclaims the security properties." Fatal gap = the **meta-oracle layer**: CIP-8 doesn't secure who reports ground truth, how competing feeds are handled, or how oracle capture is detected — "frozen criteria prevent silent rule edits, but they do not make the external fact source neutral." Second: NOTARY can **manufacture false trust** unless every surface labels results "claimed, not verified." "Resolver holds no position" needs a concrete conflict model (affiliates, correlated incentives, provider exposure, delayed/bribed positions) — "a governance promise, not a protocol property." Amendment hatch gameable unless tied to public-information timestamps. *Explicitly:* "the notary-first kernel can be ratified as a limited tamper-evident claim log," but CIP-8 *as "The Accountability Ledger"* needs the conditions first.
- **V3 (Hermes) — HOLDS:** systematic per-vector scoring. Attack 1 "real gap, but bounded — damage is user misinterpretation, not protocol failure → needs a `NOT_VERIFIED` flag." Attack 2 "deepest gap, but not unique to CIP-8 — the general oracle problem; CIP-8 *reduces* the surface (notary needs none; resolution narrows to a frozen question)"; sharpened it: **the *choice* of primary source is itself political** — conflicting official sources, or the designated source 404s — "freezing the criteria doesn't freeze the *data* they reference." Attack 3 "spec gap — fix with a signed position declaration + penalty." Attack 4 "fixable — cryptographic timestamp + public notice + **narrower-only** amendments." Attack 5 "must be specified before v0.1 — challenge triggers forced decryption or forfeit." Bottom line: "specification additions, not architectural rebuilds; the core design survives."

## Outcome — five non-negotiable invariants folded into [[CIP-8]] §9.8

Union of V1/V2/V3, deduped, promoted from "open item" to enforceable invariant (the move that satisfies V2's FAILS condition):

1. **NI-8a** — every NOTARY record labeled `NOT_VERIFIED`; guarantee is authorship+timing+non-repudiation only, never content-truth.
2. **NI-8b** — evidence commitments have teeth: challenge → disclose within a bounded window or **forfeit**; unrevealed = **zero evidentiary weight**; **no privileged decryptor**.
3. **NI-8c** — resolver no-position is a **signed, penalized declaration** for validator *and provider*; spec states [[CIP-1]] diversity stops concentration, not shared-incentive alignment.
4. **NI-8d** — RESOLUTION gated behind a frozen **ground-truth-source policy** (handles conflicting sources, 404s, source-choice politics; pluralized feeds); weak/capturable feed ⇒ **NOTARY-only fallback** (inherits [[CIP-7]] NI-6). The notary kernel needs no oracle, so the deepest risk is structurally deferred to the last gated stage.
5. **NI-8e** — amendments are cryptographically timestamped, public-noticed, and **narrower-only**; "before facts known" is judged against public-information timestamps; genuinely-ambiguous criteria resolve **INDETERMINATE**, never force-read.

Testnet gates extended (G3 labeling, G4 source-policy gate, G6 evidence-teeth + amendment-discipline). The recurring lesson holds a sixth time: capture laundered through the gap between intent and check — here, "tamper-proof record" mistaken for "verified truth," and "frozen criteria" mistaken for "neutral facts." The notary kernel (oracle-free, the part V2 agrees is ratifiable today) is exactly the right [[CIP-0]]-style KERNEL_FIRST slice to build next.
