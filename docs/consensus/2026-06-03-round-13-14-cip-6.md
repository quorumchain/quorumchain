## Round 13 — solvency model

**Options:** `ENDOWMENT` · `DYNAMIC_PRICING` · `HYBRID`
**Ballot hash:** `458780f9140d43e96bdf6654dbca8c75dde81fa4ef8dbdc9cf2b546d042a0dd2`
**Result:** ✅ **HYBRID, 3/3.**

- **V1 (Claude):** Must survive the regimes that kill the chain, not just the happy one. DYNAMIC_PRICING evaporates at zero usage — when token price (and capture cost) is lowest — and death-spirals when models are expensive. ENDOWMENT is robust but capital-inefficient and absent at bootstrap. HYBRID: per-verdict pricing carries marginal cost in steady state; a reserve funds the diversity floor through droughts.
- **V2 (Codex):** Dynamic pricing needed so usage doesn't subsidize losses, but fails the zero-usage case; pure endowment leaves an early-bootstrap security gap. Hybrid covers both: marginal cost per verdict, reserve preserves the ≥3 diverse-validator floor when usage and price collapse.
- **V3 (Hermes):** ENDOWMENT fails at bootstrap (no reserve on day one → capture before it builds). DYNAMIC_PRICING is "the trap option" (can't pay standing validators at zero usage + death spiral). HYBRID satisfies the constraint across all states and gives the right separation: **"diversity is a public good funded by the reserve; marginal cost is a private good funded by per-use fees."**

## Round 14 — validator compensation

**Options:** `PROTOCOL_FUNDED` · `SELF_FUNDED_STAKE` · `HYBRID_POI`
**Ballot hash:** `ed09c1597a9438decf5108eade40352bfae86fa343d1604939237b1bcfdf290c`
**Result:** ✅ **HYBRID_POI, 3/3.**

- **V1 (Claude):** Diversity is the security model, so the barrier to being a distinct-family validator must stay low — while honesty needs skin in the game. SELF_FUNDED_STAKE prices out diversity (only wealthy operators qualify). PROTOCOL_FUNDED has no skin in the game and invites over-claiming. HYBRID_POI: reimburse *verified* inference (proof-of-inference, CIP-0) to keep the pool wide + stake rewards/slashing (proven wrongness/collusion only, never honest dissent) for honesty.
- **V2 (Codex):** Composes with round 13 — verified inference cost is the public-good diversity expense (low barrier), stake + challenge/collusion slashing preserve alignment, proof-of-inference closes PROTOCOL_FUNDED's over-claiming, avoids SELF_FUNDED_STAKE's diversity failure.
- **V3 (Hermes):** PROTOCOL_FUNDED fails honesty (over-claiming, pays dishonest = honest); SELF_FUNDED_STAKE fails diversity (capital barrier); HYBRID_POI satisfies all constraints at once and composes cleanly with round 13 (public-good diversity from reserve, private-good cost per *verified* use).
