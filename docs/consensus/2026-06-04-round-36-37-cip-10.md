## Round 37 — Red-team

**Convergent finding:** the architecture is coherent, but the capture-resistance guarantees **invert on the hardest slots**, and several guards were named without being made mechanical. V2's FAILS and V3's HOLDS list nearly identical required conditions; the disagreement is only "specification gaps solvable pre-activation" (V1/V3) vs "load-bearing, not survived as written" (V2).

### Attacks
- **V1 (Claude) — HOLDS (conditional):** the **compounding thin-slot inversion** — an expensive/proprietary slot has few/one operators, so its draw is **predictable (bribable)**, it's the **NI-5 freeze concentration point**, AND **structurally indispensable → unslashable** (can't evict without breaching the floor): the whole weak surface clusters on the hardest models. Plus: provenance is **self-attested** (frozen taxonomy rests on unverifiable claims); the **randomness beacon** imports either an external trust root or the sequencer-grind surface; the **graduation auditor** is the most powerful unspecified lever; **two-tier dependency leakage** (judges relying on the open infra tier for weights/ballot data).
- **V2 (Codex) — FAILS (the dissent):** "the capture path is through the *diversity machinery itself*." Scarcity-weighting "concentrates economic and governance leverage exactly where decentralization is weakest." The frozen taxonomy is "a privileged chokepoint" (self-attested provenance can't distinguish real independence from distillation/wrappers; a verifier becomes the real admission authority). Randomness "not enough to save this" if the sequencer can grind/delay/reorder. The graduation gate has "the same meta-governance failure" — several criteria are "judgment-heavy" so "whoever certifies them becomes a privileged authority unless the measurements are fully mechanical and fork-enforceable." The infra tier "is also not harmless — dependency capture can become verdict capture." Required, load-bearing conditions: **no single-provider/proprietary judgment slots, independently reproducible provenance proofs, non-discretionary taxonomy admission, multi-source ungrindable randomness with forced inclusion, mechanical graduation criteria, infra dependency hardening.** "Without them, the proposal's main capture-resistance claims do not survive the adversarial case."
- **V3 (Hermes) — HOLDS:** systematic per-vector. Thin-slot inversion "the hardest — a subsidized monopolist is still a monopolist; SEL-4 addresses liveness, not capture," mitigated (not eliminated) by 1-vote/slot cap + [[CIP-5]] exit — "an honest risk, not a hidden flaw." Sharpened the **taxonomy dilemma**: frozen ⇒ new families can't enter without amendment ⇒ if the panel amends it's a **privileged registrar (the D10 anti-pattern)**, if it can't the taxonomy **ossifies** → resolve with **criterion-based provenance admission, not panel discretion.** Randomness "solvable (drand + anti-grind / forced inclusion)." Graduation criteria "not algorithmically verifiable as written" → must be computationally checkable or a certifier becomes privileged; DEC-2 is a "trilemma: graduate unsafely / recentralize / halt." Infra capture "requires explicit decoupling of log authority from infra operators." Bottom line: "**specification gaps, not architectural impossibilities** — the framework holds if the guards are filled before activation."

## Outcome — five non-negotiable invariants folded into [[CIP-10]] §9.8

Union of V1/V2/V3, deduped, promoted from open item to enforceable invariant (satisfying V2's FAILS condition):

1. **NI-10a** — thin-slot hard rule: minimum operators/slot; a single-operator/proprietary slot can **never be decisive**, is down-weighted, capped at 1 vote/slot, flagged LOW-ASSURANCE. (the compounding-inversion fix)
2. **NI-10b** — **criterion-based, reproducible** provenance admission (not panel/registrar discretion → neither privileged registrar nor ossified); unverifiable provenance ⇒ LOW-ASSURANCE + elevated correlation-eviction.
3. **NI-10c** — multi-source/panel-produced **ungrindable randomness + forced inclusion**; sequencer cannot grind/delay/reorder; stall ⇒ **freeze**, never a grindable fallback.
4. **NI-10d** — **mechanical, fork-enforceable** graduation criteria; no discretionary certifier; wrongful graduation reversible via [[CIP-5]]; DEC-2 resolves to **halt-or-extend, never graduate-unsafe.**
5. **NI-10e** — **no integrity dependency on the open tier**: judges hold own weights + ballot data; infra cannot censor/delay PoI, control the [[CIP-3]] log, or front-run selection.

Testnet gates extended (G6 fork-reversible graduation, G7 thin-slot + infra hardening). The recurring lesson holds a **seventh** time — here the gap is that the diversity *machinery itself* concentrates capture on the slots where diversity is hardest; NI-10a/b close it by refusing decisiveness to the slots the design cannot actually decentralize.
