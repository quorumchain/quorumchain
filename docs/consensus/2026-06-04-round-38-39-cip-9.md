## Outcome — five non-negotiable invariants folded into [[CIP-9]] §9.8

Union of V1/V2/V3, deduped, promoted to enforceable invariant (satisfying V2's FAILS), binding before v0.2/v0.3:

1. **NI-9a** — every claim version records the **panel composition + diversity state** that produced it; no CONSENSUS without the panel-state receipt (captured-era records identifiable).
2. **NI-9b** — **external-anchor reputation only**: accuracy scored against ground truth *external* to the panel; panel-resolved-only claims move **no** reputation (kills agreement-laundering; the NI-6 boundary). *The crux fix.*
3. **NI-9c** — standing is **computed by auditable criteria, never panel-assigned**; unrankable (unverifiable) stances show raw plurality, no "fringe" demotion.
4. **NI-9d** — **provenance-distinguishable forks** + discovery/selection norms; no silent brand-capture.
5. **NI-9e** — **bonded, provenance-anchored stance admission**; no privileged editor gate, no free spam.

Testnet gates extended (G7 no-agreement-laundering, G8 no-editorial-demotion + captured-consensus visibility). The recurring lesson holds an **eighth** time — at the knowledge layer it is the deepest yet: "accuracy" is the *intent*, "agreement with the resolver" is the *check*, and they are identical wherever the resolver is the only source of truth. NI-9b severs them by requiring the truth to come from outside the panel — or moving no reputation at all.
